## Write a short risk note for a company holding assets in USD, Gold or RMB/CNY. 

In today’s world, China and the USA are two highly tangled economies and two trade partners.
As it was discussed in the previous question, currencies are less volatile in the short-term perspective.
But in the long-term perspective, currencies are less reliable.

For China’s economy, a strong local currency is deadly because it will make their products less competitive.
Gold is falling after a period of being on the rise; do not feel it would change in the short term.

Risks:
1- US President Trump.
- Trade wars with China can affect USD and CNY.
- A stronger RMB/CNY can reduce China’s export competitiveness, so it has no potential to grow significantly.

2- USD currency as a reserve currency.
- For now, USD is still a reserve currency. But it must be monitored closely.
- After a new currency occurs, the reserve will become weaker.

3- Gold seems least risky, but grows with inflation.
- For the past 40 years, gold has been considered a long-term hedge against inflation.
- After the USA/Israel/Iran war, gold spiked and is now returning to previous prices.
- Price can be volatile in the short and medium term.

Summary:
Short-term USD is the best option.
Long-term Gold is the best option.

RMB/CNY is not a good option for now, but may be an option for divercification
