### Annas' question
## Give three practical recommendations for investors, companies, or policymakers based on your findings.

#### Recommendation for investors:
Diversify your investments.
Investors should maintain diversified portfolios across different asset classes and geographic regions. A combination of equities, gold, and other assets can help reduce exposure to currency fluctuations and geopolitical risks. Companies with global operations may provide additional resilience during periods of economic uncertainty.
- I would recommend individual investors to invest in gold and US companies with access to global market

#### Recommendation for companies:
Companies should diversify both their markets and supply chains. Expanding operations across multiple regions can reduce dependence on a single economy, currency, or trading partner. Geographic diversification may improve resilience against trade disputes, regulatory changes, and local economic downturns.

#### Recommendation for policymakers:
Policymakers should focus on maintaining economic stability, predictable regulations, and sustainable long-term growth. Investments in infrastructure, innovation, and productivity can strengthen economic competitiveness and increase investor confidence. Consistent and transparent policies help build trust among domestic and international stakeholders.
