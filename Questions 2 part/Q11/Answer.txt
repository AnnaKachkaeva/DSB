Sanctions, alternative settlement systems, and BRICS Pay discussions are important for currency market analysis because they influence how countries conduct international payments and settle trade. When countries face sanctions or seek to reduce reliance on USD-based payment systems, they may explore using local currencies, RMB/CNY, gold, or alternative payment networks.This shift can:

Change the demand for major currencies
Influence exchange rate behavior
